💬 Continuous Feedback
Open each feedback card on SuccessFactors → Continuous Feedback → Received, copy the full card text, and paste it below to auto-fill.
1. Go to hcm41.sapsf.com → Continuous Feedback → Received
2. Click "View more" on each feedback card to expand it
3. Select all text on the card (sender name, date, What went well, What could be improved)
4. Press Ctrl+C to copy
5. Paste into the box below and click Parse & Fill
💬 Paste & Parse Feedback from SuccessFactors
Copy text from each feedback card and paste here to auto-fill
⚡ Fastest way — copy all at once: 1. On the SuccessFactors Feedback list page, open the first feedback detail
2. Press Ctrl+A then Ctrl+C to copy the full page
3. Go back, open the next feedback, and press Ctrl+A → Ctrl+C again
4. Paste all of them together below — the tool will split and fill each one automatically
